Welcome to Trivia Blanca!

Rules/Things to Know

1: You can use any resources you want, including other people. However, nobody is obligated to answer you or tell the truth!

2: The answers are not case (or space) sensitive, but they do need to be spelled exactly correctly, or be in the correct format. I will do my best to make that formatting obvious, but if you think you have the right answer and it isn't working, feel free to contact me!

3: Everything on the page can potentially be a clue, including the URL. This means that browsers like Safari might be harder to use, because they don't show the entire URL!

4: There is no way to save progress. When you get to a new level, it may be worth bookmarking or writing down the URL so you can get back to it if you need to!

5: There are some obscure questions, you will not know the answers to all of them. Asking other people is recommended if you get stuck!

6: If you get really stuck, feel free to ask for a hint!
